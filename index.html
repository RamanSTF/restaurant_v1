Our History
Like any other distinctive place, we have our own, special history. The idea for the restaurant came to the founders out of the blue. While walking through the woods, the creator of our restaurant got stuck hundreds of kilometers away from a populated area. Away from civilization and communication, they had to set up a rudimentary household for a while, to find and prepare their own food.
